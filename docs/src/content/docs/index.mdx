## What it is

Zorite is a daily journal for your desktop. It works like Logseq — every day is
a page, notes link to each other, and everything is searchable — but you type
into it like a Word document, not an outliner. Each line is just editable
Markdown: click in to edit the raw text, click away and it re-renders.

Your notes are Markdown rows in a local SQLite database; images and PDFs live
beside it as files. There's no cloud and no account — everything stays on your
machine. Built in Rust with [gpui](https://www.gpui.rs/) (Zed's GPU-accelerated
UI framework), it stays immediate even with tens of thousands of pages.

## What you get

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Journal & pages" icon="document">
    An infinite, reverse-chronological feed of days. `[[wiki-links]]`, `#tags`,
    and backlinks; `Foo::Bar` namespaces and sub-pages; a `/` command palette
    and templates. [Journal & pages](/zorite/usage/journal/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Whiteboards" icon="pencil">
    A freeform infinite canvas alongside the text journal — pen, shapes, text,
    images, and page-card embeds, with reusable templates.
    [Whiteboards](/zorite/usage/whiteboards/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="PDF viewer" icon="open-book">
    Link or drop a PDF to open it in a page-virtualized viewer: zoom, a TOC,
    drag-to-highlight markup, and password-protected files.
    [PDF & images](/zorite/usage/pdf/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full-text search" icon="magnifier">
    A trigram FTS5 index over titles and content, with type-aware results for
    pages, PDFs, images, and whiteboards. [Search](/zorite/usage/search/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Import from Logseq" icon="random">
    Bring in a Logseq graph — pages, journals, assets, namespaces, tasks,
    aliases, whiteboards, and favorites.
    [Import from Logseq](/zorite/usage/import/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-platform" icon="laptop">
    macOS, Windows, and Linux from a single Rust binary. GPU-accelerated, native
    title-bar treatment per platform. [Requirements](/zorite/reference/requirements/).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Get started

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Install" icon="rocket">
    Homebrew, winget, Scoop, and direct downloads. Package managers land with
    the first stable release; betas are on the Releases page.
    [Install instructions](/zorite/install/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Write your journal" icon="pencil">
    Click into today, type Markdown, link pages with `[[…]]`, and let backlinks
    build themselves. [Journal & pages](/zorite/usage/journal/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Annotate PDFs" icon="open-book">
    Drop a PDF onto a note, then drag to highlight and jump between your note and
    the page. [PDF & images](/zorite/usage/pdf/).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Make it yours" icon="setting">
    Pick a built-in theme, choose Light / Dark / Auto, or drop in a custom
    `.json` theme. [Themes](/zorite/customize/themes/).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
